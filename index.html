Q: How do I add a pet?

A: Click the "➕ Add" button in the top header or click the "Add Pet" chip on the setup screen to enter pet details.

Q: Where is my data stored?

A: All your pet logs are stored securely in your web browser's local storage (localStorage). Export your data regularily using the Data Management section to backup.
